About BoyNextDoor

BOYNEXTDOOR (can be shorten as BND) is an South Korean boy group under KOZ Entertainment (which is a sublabel of Hybe Entertainment) who debuted on May 30, 2023 with the single album “WHO!“. The members of this group consist of Jaehyun, Sungho, Riwoo, Taesan, Leehan and Woonhak. The members in BND all have great charisma and they always strive for the best in their performances. All the members are close and like family despite the short time period they have trained and debuted together for. BND have already proved themselves worthy of fame as they have already broken some records in their first week of debut.

History

Predebut

  • On January 3, 2022, KOZ announced that they are holding auditions for their new boy group on HYBE Label's YouTube channel.

  • On June 15, KOZ launched a teen rapper audition called 'KOZ Teen Rapper Audition: U18 in the Zungle' in hopes of discovering members of their upcoming boy group. It was also revealed the group would be making their debut in 2023.

  • On August 4, Zico guested on SBS Power FM's Two O'Clock Escape Cultwo Show and revealed that the agency's upcoming boy group would not be hip-hop as they want to create a group that isn't limited to a specific genre.

  • On February 28, 2023, SPOTV News reported that the group will be making their debut in May. KOZ Entertainment announced that the exact date is yet to be confirmed although they are expected to debut in the first half of the year.

Debut with 'WHO!'

  • On April 3, KOZ Entertainment started a 56-day countdown through their official website. On April 4, KOZ confirmed that their new boy group would be preparing to debut on May 30, 2023.

  • On April 17, the group's logo motion was released through the HYBE Labels YouTube channel, revealing that its name would be BOYNEXTDOOR. On the same day, the group's social networks were opened. Hours later it was confirmed that the group would have six members.

  • On May 1, KOZ Entertainment started the countdown for the group's debut. On May 3, it was announced that the group would debut with their first single album titled "Who!".

  • On May 3, it was announced that the group would debut with their first single album titled "Who!". On May 5, the names of the six members of the group were revealed through their social networks.
